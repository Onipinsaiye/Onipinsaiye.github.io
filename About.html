About Us

We are a fast growing Company committed to the growth and development of the rural communities through profitable agricultural practices.

Our Vision and Mission

Our Vision...

Klaro vision is dedicated to addressing three major issues in rural communities. To begin, our Vision is intended to reduce the involvement of able-bodied youth in criminal activities and terrorism by assisting them in developing a profitable career in poultry farming. Secondly to assist the average poultry farmer in rural communities make more money and finally, to aid the development of rural communities economies.

Our Mission...

To build the rural economies to become a safe and attractive haven for investors.
