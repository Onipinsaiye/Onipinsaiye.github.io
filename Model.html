Our Business Model

Klaro busines model is designed to address key problems mitigating the proper growth and development of the rural economies. We believe by developing the rural communities, we can reduce the mass rural-urban migration, create more jobs for the rural dwellers, flatten the poverty curve of the rural communities and make the rural regions a safe haven for investors.

Rural Employement


We leverage agricultrue to provide jobs for the rural populace.

Rural Wealth Creation


We strive to enrich the rural communities through agriculture.

Rural Security


We believe by creating jobs for the rural community we will reduce
cases of crimine and terrorism and this will make the rural community
a safe haven for investors to do business.
